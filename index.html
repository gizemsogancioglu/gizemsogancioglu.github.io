Bio

I'm a Ph.D. student at Social and Affective Computing Group, Department of Information and Computing Sciences at Utrecht University. I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Computer Engineering Department at Hacettepe University and Bogazici University respectively. After my master studies, I worked at different companies as NLP Research Engineer for ~5 years. Currently, I pursue a Ph.D. on the topic of explainability in the ML applications of mental health domain. I am also highly interested in problems concerning fairness in ML algorithms.

Research Interests

  • Affective computing
  • Explainability in machine Learning
  • Algorithmic fairness
  • Machine learning in mental health
